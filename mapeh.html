I am Human, I am a Woman

by Simran Sohal, November 10, 2022

Know More:

The Music notes imply "Music towards enlightenment", wherein intellectual and cultural movement were introduced. The colored patches reflect the symbolic meaning of the colors reflect the phycology of humans; red/pink symbolizes love, passion and drama; yellow symbolizes optimism; green symbolizes growth and lastly; blue symbolizes trust and sadness. For the third eye and stiches on the mouth, I wanted to imply how humans can see or feel beyond what is said/shown/seen, and approach these situations. And lastly, the colorful neck/throat simply symbolizes the mixture of emotions, speech and thoughts.
